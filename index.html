When will MapleChat launch?

MapleChat is currently in private testing with early users. Public access will roll out in phases so we can scale smoothly.

How will my data be protected?

Messages are encrypted in transit and stored on Canadian infrastructure, with controls to manage or delete your data.

Will all content be Canadian?

MapleChat is designed for Canadians first, but you’ll still be able to connect with friends and family living abroad.

Which platforms will MapleChat support?

The initial release will focus on iOS and Android, with the web app following once the core experience is polished.

Can I reserve my username?

Early testers will be able to claim usernames during onboarding, and we’ll share details before wider launch.
